The Abhivyakti is the national cultural event of IIITN. Every year , it happens with great enthusiasm where a particular theme is adopted ,college premises are decorated and many exciting events are organized.This fest provides great opportunity for students to showcase their talent apart from academics


Featured Events

BIG ROAR

This is a debate competition wherein participants have to express their views on the given topic. To develop public speaking skills, critical thinking abilities and to promote the exchange of ideas and skills between members, Big Roar is open for all the other local colleges too.

TO THE BEAT

With the aim of honing the dancing skills of the students, this event is all about dancing. Solo paricipants with classical,western,traditional or any other dance form are allowed to perform. In the final round, the selected participants again undergo dance battle and the judges decide the winner.

VYAKTITVA

Beauty captures the eye,Personality captures the heart! This is what the event defines about.Its a personality contest where you have to present yourself in such a way that you will be chosen the as the best one among all.Every small thing about you, the way you walk,talk and behave is judged.

ABHIVYAKTI'S GOT TALENT

This event is best to show multiple talents like singing,dancing,stand-up, playing instrument,beat-boxing and much more. If you think you have any special,unique talent to perform then AGT is the best platform!

OPEN MIC

This event gives a platform for students to shed their fears and perform anything in solo or in group.There is no such restriction regarding theme and rules.The only purpose of this event is to have fun,entertainment and rejoice.Also some professional stand-up comedians,singers are invited for performances from outside and ending with DJ night.

KAVYANJALI

A poet is a person who is passionately in love with the languages. The event is for them who are all excited about the art of poetry! There is no language barrier for the poems to present but it should be theme-based. The winner is decided on basis of Content of poem and overall expressions.

CRAFTIX

Being creative is not a hobby,it is a way of life! Crafix gives you a chance to showcase your best art creations. You need to post it on the page at specified time and the one with maximum likes and votes is awarded.

TALK MASTERS

Talk masters is test your oratory skills. A topic will be given and the participants need to present it in a given time limit. How quick,alert and how relevant you speak is judged in this event. All the participants need to speak in English only.

ANTARA

For all the singers and music lovers,Antara event gives you an opportunity! There is no language barrier for contestants to sing a song in the competition. Winner is decided on basis of your overall stage performance,vocal range and quality and rythmic interpretation.


Pre-Events:


Samarth

Abhivyakti, the cultural fest organized a pre-event of "SAMARTH" with the aim of ensuring the support of society for the specially abled children. Samarth basically gives a platform for these children coming from various schools to showcase their talent like singing, dancing, playing instruments,beat-boxing and much more. This event was organized at Vasantrao Deshpande Hall which was open for IIITN students as well as for the local people. The funds collected through their entry tickets were donated to the NGO on behalf of the institute. After their performances, all the participants and their teachers were felicitated with gifts as a small token of love, followed by huge round of applause and appreciation coming from audience and making this event a huge success.

Cycle Rally

Cycle rally with motto of "Cycling towards the better Future" , a pre-event of Abhivyakti was organized this year. The rally was flagged off and it started from college premises at 7 AM covering few kilometers of city roads. The chief purpose behind the Rally was to spread health awareness among the students as Cycling is one best exercises for a fit, healthy and wealthy life. The rally was organised to spread a message of conservation of fuels,energy and against Air Pollution. Most of the students actively participated and showed great enthusiasm for this event.
